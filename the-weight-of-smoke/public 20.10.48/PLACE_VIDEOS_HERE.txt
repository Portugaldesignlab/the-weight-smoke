Drop the two hero background videos into THIS folder (the project's /public):

    azetc.mp4
    azetec-01.mp4

Vite serves everything in /public at the site root, so the Hero loads them
as "/azetc.mp4" and "/azetec-01.mp4". No import or path change needed —
just place the files here and run `npm run dev`.

If you rename the files, update the VIDEO_A / VIDEO_B constants at the top of
src/components/Hero.jsx to match.

You can delete this note once the videos are in place.
